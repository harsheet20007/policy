1. Purpose

This policy outlines the principles and guidelines for integrating Google Drive with our ERP website, ensuring secure and ethical usage of user data while complying with Google’s API policies and data protection regulations.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all users of the ERP website who utilize the Google Drive integration feature, as well as the developers and administrators responsible for managing the integration.

3. Consent and Authorization

  • Users must explicitly consent to allow the ERP website to access their Google Drive account through OAuth 2.0 authentication.
  • The ERP website will request the minimum permissions required to fulfill its intended functionality.
  • Users can revoke access at any time via their Google Account settings.

4. Data Access and Usage

  • The ERP website will only access files and folders specified by the user.
  • No unauthorized or broad-scope data access will occur.
  • Accessed data will solely be used for purposes directly related to ERP operations, such as document storage and sharing, automated file backups, and generating reports.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • No files from Google Drive will be stored on the ERP’s servers unless explicitly required for operational purposes and agreed upon by the user.
  • Secure encryption protocols will be used for any data transmission or storage.
  • User data will not be shared with third parties unless mandated by law.

6. Token Management

  • OAuth tokens will be securely stored and encrypted.
  • Refresh tokens will only be used to renew access when necessary.
  • Expired or revoked tokens will be promptly deleted.

7. Compliance

The integration will adhere to Google’s API Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Regular audits will be conducted to identify and mitigate potential risks.

8. Policy Updates

This policy will be reviewed and updated annually or as required to reflect changes in technology, user needs, or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes.

9. Contact Information

For questions or concerns about this policy, users can contact:

  • Support Team: support@erpwebsite.com
  • Compliance Officer: compliance@erpwebsite.com
